What You'll Learn: 6-Day Breakdown

Day 1: Greetings & Introductions

Say hello, goodbye, and introduce yourself clearly.

Day 2: Talk About Your Spanish Skills

Day 3: Order Food & Drinks

Navigate menus and order confidently in cafés and restaurants.

Day 5: Check Into a Hotel

Use polite phrases and understand common hotel questions.
